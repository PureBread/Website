The Game

Llammigration is a game about leading a herd of llamas on a journey through South America. Lead your herd through the journey to a new land safely by managing their resources effectively.

Features

  • strategic gameplay
  • south american inspired music
  • choice based events
  • destinations
  • casual & challenge mode

Llammigration takes place in South America. Similar to Oregon Trail, an educational game which inspired the development of Llammigration, you can learn about aspects of South American culture. Each of the destinations in the game are based on major landmarks or cities which exemplify the diversity of the continent, with a soundtrack heavily inspired by authentic South American music. Other interesting facts about the wildlife, geography, and culture can also be found in random events throughout the game.

In addition to being educational, Llammigration is suitable for a family audience, specifically targeted at students ages 6-16. The gameplay mechanics are deliberately designed to be as simple as possibleso that young children will not find the game overly challenging due to physical constraints, while still providing a suitable mental challenge through the resource-based gameplay.
